Chasing Aroma's Philosophy

Driven by Scents, Guided by Soul

Chasing Aroma was born from a simple obsession: that the smell of freshly roasted specialty coffee represents the absolute pinnacle of morning comfort. Founded in 2021, we set out to bridge the gap between world-class café environments and domestic kitchens.

We source single-origin organic coffee beans directly from ethical family micro-farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Sumatra. Each roast is dialled in to maximize sweetness and preserve delicate floral and fruity notes. But we didn't want the story to end at our espresso bar.

Empowering Home Baristas

We share our proprietary recipes openly, ensuring anyone can brew the exact same standard at home.

Zero-Waste Footprint

All of our packaging is 100% compostable, and we repurpose used coffee grinds into localized organic fertilizers.
